Pulse — Health App

Role

Product Designer & UX Lead

Tools

Figma, Principle, Zeplin, Lottie

Year

2024

Overview

Pulse is a health and wellness app designed for people who want to stay in tune with their body—without the noise of overcrowded feature sets. The design philosophy was radical simplicity: remove every element that doesn't serve the user's core goal.

The onboarding flow uses progressive disclosure to ease users into habit formation. Data visualizations were custom-designed to be beautiful at a glance and insightful on deeper inspection. The design system is built on an 8pt grid with a warm, accessible color palette.

The result is an app that feels like a trusted companion rather than a productivity tool.
